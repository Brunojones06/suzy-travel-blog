About Suzie

A girl, a bag, and a relentless curiosity about what's around the next corner.

Lisbon, Portugal — Home Base

Hi, I'm Suzie.

Travel writer · Storyteller · Perpetual wanderer

It started with a one-way ticket to Lisbon that I bought on a Tuesday afternoon when I really should have been doing something more responsible. I had a small bag, a rough idea of what I was doing, and absolutely no plan beyond the first week. That was four years ago. I never quite went back.

I grew up in a small town where the most exciting thing that happened each year was the summer fair. I always had this feeling that the world was enormous and I was only seeing a very tiny corner of it. Books helped, and then travel did the rest. The first time I landed somewhere where I didn't speak the language and couldn't read the signs, I felt something click into place.

This blog is my attempt to write down what travelling actually feels like — not the filtered, highlight-reel version, but the real thing. The bad flights and the great ones. The meals that changed me and the ones I probably shouldn't have eaten. The places that were exactly what the photos promised, and the places that were something else entirely and better for it.

I travel because the world is genuinely extraordinary, and I don't think we remind ourselves of that enough. I travel because every place I go teaches me something I didn't know I needed to learn. And honestly, I travel because sitting still has never come naturally to me — and somewhere along the way, I stopped trying to fight it.

— Suzie x
